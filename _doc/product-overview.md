# Product Overview

## Core Concept
A free, self-hosted digital product storefront page — modeled after SellHub (sellhub.cx) — built so sellers can showcase and sell digital goods online with zero platform commissions or monthly fees.

## The Problem
Paid digital storefront platforms (SellHub, Sellix, Shoppy) charge commissions on every sale and monthly subscription fees, eroding margins and locking sellers into someone else's platform and brand experience.

## The Solution
A self-owned product page with the same polished, conversion-optimized UX as SellHub — product hero gallery, price display, Buy Now CTA, product details, trust signals, and customer reviews — hosted freely with no middleman and no fees.

## Target Users
- Independent digital product creators and sellers (templates, software, digital accounts, presets, files, courses)
- Developers, indie makers, and entrepreneurs wanting full ownership of their storefront
- Current users of paid platforms (SellHub, Sellix, Shoppy) looking to eliminate platform fees

## Core Features
- **Product Hero** — image gallery showcasing the product prominently
- **Pricing & Buy CTA** — clear price display with a prominent "Buy Now" button wired to a payment flow
- **Product Details** — description, what's included, file specs, and digital delivery information
- **Trust Signals** — payment method badges, security indicators, and stock/availability status
- **Reviews & Social Proof** — customer ratings and testimonials displayed prominently
- **Urgency & Scarcity Indicators** — stock counts and limited-availability signals that drive conversion

## Positioning
The free, self-hosted alternative to SellHub. No commissions. No monthly fees. Full ownership of the buyer experience.

## Brand Tone
Clean, modern, conversion-focused. Professional and trustworthy without being corporate or flashy.

## MVP Scope
A single, polished product page (storefront listing) with the full SellHub-style UX: hero, pricing, product details, trust signals, reviews, and a buy flow. Self-hosted, zero platform fees.
